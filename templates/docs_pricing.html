Pricing

Free Tier

Get started with PatternFoundry at no cost. The Free tier includes:

  • 3 simulator sessions per day
  • 5 preset chart characters
  • Basic indicators (SMA, EMA, RSI)
  • 1 saved session slot
  • No custom indicators
  • No Chart Designer access

Practice Tier — $9.99/month

For serious practice and skill development:

  • Unlimited simulator sessions
  • All preset chart characters
  • All built-in indicators
  • 10 saved session slots
  • Custom indicator editor
  • 5 saved custom indicators
  • Chart Designer with 5 custom character slots
  • Session export/import
  • All drawing tools

Edge Tier — $19.99/month

The complete PatternFoundry experience for professional development:

  • Everything in Practice, plus:
  • Unlimited saved sessions
  • Unlimited custom indicators
  • Unlimited custom characters
  • Advanced microstructure controls
  • Tick Engine Preview & debug panel
  • DOM (Depth of Market) panel
  • Priority session generation
  • Early access to new features
