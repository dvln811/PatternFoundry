Marketing Plan (introvert-compatible)

What We're Selling

PatternFoundry is a free trading practice simulator with realistic microstructure, 14 instruments, persistent performance tracking, and an Iron Man challenge mode. The product thesis: deliberate practice with instant feedback builds trading skill faster than paper trading on live markets.

Core value proposition: Unlimited practice sessions with real market microstructure, zero risk, zero cost. Track your progress across hundreds of sessions. Know your actual win rate, drawdown profile, and streak patterns before putting real money at risk.

Target Audience

  • Prop firm candidates — Want to practice before paying $100-$300 for another eval
  • Beginner futures traders — Learning ES/NQ/CL price action without losing money
  • Screen time seekers — Traders who want more "reps" outside market hours
  • Strategy testers — Want to practice a specific setup across many sessions
  • Data-driven traders — Want to track actual stats (win rate, drawdown, streaks) over time

Positioning

"The driving range for traders."

We're not a backtest platform. We're not a paper trading account. We're a practice environment with instant session generation, realistic order book dynamics, and honest performance tracking.

NOT: "AI trading signals" / "get funded fast" / "guaranteed profits"

YES: "Practice deliberately. Know your actual stats. Improve before risking real capital."

Content Angles

  • "I practiced X sessions of Y — here's the data"
    Screenshots of stats. Let numbers speak. Minimal narration.
  • "Built a free trading simulator — Show HN"
    3 sentences + link. Technical audience will explore if it's good.
  • "Why win rate alone is meaningless"
    Screenshot of streak/drawdown data. Myth-bust format works on X.
  • "Free alternative to $300 replay software"
    SEO target. Landing page + comparison angle.
  • Silent screen recordings
    30-sec clip: open sim, take a trade, see stats. No talking. No editing.

Rules of Engagement

  • PRINCIPLE — Build in public, don't perform in public
  • PRINCIPLE — The product IS the marketing. Ship good work, show it exists.
  • ALWAYS — Lead with data/screenshots, not pitches
  • ALWAYS — Show honest results (wins AND losses)
  • NEVER — Force yourself to "engage" daily. Burnout kills projects.
  • NEVER — Promise results, use guru language, fake scarcity
  • NEVER — Cold DM, spam links, reply-guy behavior
  • ENERGY — If a channel feels draining, drop it. Ship a feature instead.

Reddit Mod-Proof Rules

  • NEVER — Put a link in the post body. Ever. Link goes in a comment, only when asked.
  • NEVER — Post in a sub where you haven't commented for 2+ weeks first.
  • NEVER — Use promotional language ("check out my tool!", "free!", CTAs).
  • ALWAYS — Frame posts as "here's what I learned" with data. The tool is incidental.
  • ALWAYS — Use a neutral username (not brand-named).
  • ALWAYS — Have genuine comment karma in the sub before posting.
  • IF DELETED — Don't fight it. Move to the next sub. Never message mods arguing.

Competitive Landscape

  • TradingView Paper Trading — Live market only, no extra "reps" outside hours. Complex platform.
  • Forex Tester / Soft4FX — Desktop, $200-$300, replay historical data. No generation.
  • NinjaTrader Replay — Complex setup, futures-specific, steep learning curve.
  • Prop Firm Sims — Tied to expensive evals. Same live-market limitation.

Our edge: Free. Instant sessions anytime. Realistic microstructure. 14 instruments. Persistent stats. Iron Man challenge. Zero setup — open browser, start trading.

Success Metrics

  • Week 2: Reddit karma building. HN post drafted. X account ready.
  • Week 4: First Reddit value post live. 10+ signups from organic channels.
  • Month 2: 50+ registered users. Reddit post(s) still up (not deleted). 1 SEO page ranking.
  • Month 3: 100+ users. Assess what's driving signups — double down on that channel.

Track via /admin/users. Add Plausible analytics ($9/mo) after 50+ active users.

The product is free during growth phase. Focus on building something people want first. Monetization options once 100+ active users exist:

  • Premium tier ($9.99/mo) — Custom instruments, advanced stats, priority feature requests
  • Prop firm partnerships — Referral deals for practice → eval pipeline
  • Keep it free + donations — Kofi/GitHub Sponsors if community is strong enough
