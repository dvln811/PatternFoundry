Iron Man Mode

🦾 Iron Man Challenge

A persistent account challenge designed to build discipline. No resets, no do-overs — just like a real funded account.

  • Fixed starting capital — $10,000 simulated account
  • No session skipping — you must finish each session (or it counts as a flat day)
  • No cherry-picking — sessions are assigned randomly, not chosen
  • Quitting forfeits the run — if you abandon mid-challenge, stats reset
  • Drawdown limit — breach -20% and the challenge ends (like a prop firm eval)
  • Target — reach +10% ($11,000) to "pass" the challenge
  • Tracks separately — Iron Man stats don't mix with free practice stats

Status: Concept — not yet implemented. This is the design direction.
