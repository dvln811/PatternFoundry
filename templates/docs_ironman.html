Iron Man Mode

What Is Iron Man Mode?

Iron Man Mode is a structured trading challenge that tests your discipline and consistency. The goal is simple: grow your account by 10% without ever drawing down 20% from your peak balance. There are no time limits — only your ability to stay disciplined session after session.

The Challenge

Start with a fixed balance. Grow it to the +10% target before your drawdown from peak hits -20%. One session at a time, no shortcuts.

How It Works

  1. Start a Run — Go to the Stats page and begin an Iron Man challenge. Your run starts with a $10,000 balance.
  2. Trade in the Simulator — When you have an active run, the simulator automatically enters Iron Man mode. You'll see a 🔥 IRON MAN badge in the Session panel.
  3. Random Instruments — You don't get to choose your instrument. The simulator picks one at random each session. This forces you to be adaptable.
  4. Sessions Auto-Submit — When you close a session, your P&L is automatically recorded to the challenge. No manual tracking needed.
  5. Run Until End — The challenge continues until you either hit the target or breach the drawdown limit.

End Conditions

Drawdown Calculation

Drawdown is measured from your peak balance, not your starting balance. For example:

  • You start at $10,000 and grow to $10,500 — your peak is now $10,500
  • The 20% drawdown limit is calculated from your starting balance: $10,000 × 20% = $2,000
  • If your balance drops $2,000 below your peak ($10,500 → $8,500), the challenge ends
Key Detail

The drawdown percentage is measured relative to your starting balance, but tracked from your highest point. This means the higher you climb, the more room you have — but a big loss can still end the run.

Simulator Behavior During Iron Man

When an Iron Man run is active, the simulator changes in several ways:

  • 🔥 Badge visible — An orange "IRON MAN" indicator appears in the Session panel
  • Instrument locked — The instrument dropdown is disabled; a random instrument is assigned each session
  • Challenge balance used — Your session starts with the Iron Man run balance, not your regular trading account
  • Auto-submission — Closing a session automatically records the result to your active run
  • Immediate feedback — If the session ends the challenge (target or drawdown), you're notified immediately

Tracking Progress

Your Iron Man progress is visible on the Stats page:

  • Active run display with current balance, day count, drawdown percentage, and progress bar
  • Per-session breakdown (instrument, trades, wins, P&L, balance after)
  • History of past attempts with outcomes

Tips for Success

  • Small, consistent gains — You only need +10%. Don't swing for the fences.
  • Respect the drawdown — A 20% buffer feels large until you have two bad sessions in a row.
  • Adapt to the instrument — Since instruments are random, focus on reading price action rather than relying on instrument-specific habits.
  • Know when to stop — If you're tilting, close the session early or forfeit. You can always start a new attempt.

Starting & Managing Runs

Iron Man runs are managed from the Stats page:

  • Start — Click "Start Iron Man" in the Iron Man panel
  • Forfeit — Click "Forfeit" to end the run voluntarily at any time
  • History — All past attempts are listed with their outcome, duration, and final balance
