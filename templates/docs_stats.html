Performance Stats

The Stats page gives you a complete picture of your trading performance across all accounts. It's organized into three tabs: Overview, Account Details, and Iron Man.

Overview Tab

The Overview tab provides a high-level snapshot of your overall progress:

  • Aggregated Equity Curve — Your cumulative return across all accounts, normalized as a percentage of each account's starting balance. This lets you compare performance regardless of account size.
  • Quick Stats — Total sessions, trades, win rate, cumulative P/L, best and worst trading days at a glance.
  • Monthly P&L — All 12 months of the year displayed as cards. Green/red for profitable/losing months, gray for months with no activity. Click a month to see the calendar drill-down.
  • Month Detail — A calendar view showing per-day P&L for the selected month. Days with sessions are color-coded green or red.
  • Top Instruments — Your most-traded instruments ranked by session count, with win rate bars.
  • Session Distribution — A day-of-week chart showing when you tend to practice.
  • Recent Sessions — Quick list of your last 8 sessions with P&L.
  • Practice Streak — Consecutive days with at least one session, plus a full-year activity heatmap.

Account Details Tab

Drill into a specific trading account's performance:

  • Account Selector — Switch between your active account and archived accounts. Shows status, balance, and creation date.
  • Starting Balance & P/L — Dedicated cards showing the account's starting point and total profit/loss with percentage.
  • Stat Cards — Sessions, Total Trades, Win Rate, Cumulative P/L, Profit Factor, Avg Win, Avg Loss, Largest Win, Largest Loss, Max Consecutive Wins/Losses, and Avg Trade.
  • Equity Curve — Per-account cumulative P/L over sessions.
  • Drawdown Chart — Red area chart showing drawdown from peak equity. Appears after 2+ sessions.
  • Per-Instrument Breakdown — Table showing trades, win rate, P/L, average trade, and profit factor for each instrument traded on this account.
  • Session History — Full table of all sessions with date, instrument, trades, win rate, P/L, result tag, and replay link.
Tip

Click the ▶ icon next to any session in the history table to replay it in the simulator with all your original trades and drawings restored.

Iron Man Tab

The Iron Man tab manages your challenge runs. See the Iron Man Mode documentation for full details.

  • Start a Challenge — Choose from presets (Micro $5k, Standard $10k, Full $50k, Hardcore) or configure custom parameters.
  • Active Run — Live progress bar, balance, P/L %, drawdown %, and session-by-session breakdown.
  • Past Attempts — History table with result, parameters, sessions completed, and final return percentage.
  • Aggregate Metrics — Total runs, pass rate, average return, best/worst run.

Key Metrics Explained

MetricWhat It Measures
Profit FactorGross wins ÷ gross losses. Above 1.0 means you're net profitable.
Avg Win / Avg LossMean dollar amount of winning and losing trades. Compare these to assess risk/reward.
Max Consecutive Wins/LossesLongest streak of consecutive winning or losing trades.
DrawdownThe maximum peak-to-trough decline in account equity.
Win RatePercentage of trades closed in profit out of total trades.
Note

Iron Man sessions are tracked separately and do not affect your regular account balance. They appear in the Iron Man tab only.

Managing Accounts

Your stats are tied to trading accounts. Use the Settings page to reset your account (which archives the old one). Archived accounts remain visible in the Account Details tab for historical reference. Use Purge All Archived to permanently delete archived account data.
